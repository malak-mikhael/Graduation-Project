Real Madrid Academy is one of the most distinguished training academies in the world of football

It has many features that make it stand out from the rest of the academies, and some of these features include:

Experience and excellence: The Real Madrid Academy has a team of male and female coaches with extensive experience in the field of football, who work to train young players and develop their football skills in effective and innovative ways.

Infrastructure: The Real Madrid Academy features advanced infrastructure that includes grass and artificial football pitches, training and entertainment halls, nutrition, covered pitches and medical facilities, providing an ideal training environment for children and youth.

In short, the Real Madrid Academy is characterized by excellence and creativity in training young players, and provides an ideal training environment that makes it an ideal destination for anyone who wants to achieve success.

Watching Real Madrid at the Santiago Bernabéu is one of the best sporting experiences, but tickets may be too expensive for some fans. For this reason, Real Madrid seeks to motivate its fans and reduce the financial burden on them by offering many discounts and exclusive offers.

Real Madrid is currently offering a 20% discount when booking match tickets via the club's official app, which is a very tempting offer for football fans around the world. By downloading the application on their mobile phones, fans can obtain this exclusive discount with ease, by searching for the match they wish to attend and choosing the number of tickets required.

Users can use the app's discount code to get a 20% discount on the cost of tickets, and can get up to 4 tickets at the reduced discount, making this exclusive offer a great opportunity for families and friends to enjoy watching Real Madrid together at lower prices.
